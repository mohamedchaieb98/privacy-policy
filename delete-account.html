Account and Data Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from our systems, please follow the instructions below:

How to Request Deletion

Email us at water.delivery.chb@gmail.com with the subject line: Delete My Account.

Please include your registered phone number or account email in the message so we can identify your account.

What Will Happen

- Your personal information, including name, address, and phone number, will be deleted permanently.
- This action cannot be undone.
- Deletion will be processed within 7 days.

If you have questions, contact us at the same email address above.
